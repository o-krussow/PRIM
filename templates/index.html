Created by Pierce Lane, Owen Krussow, Sam Aldeguer, Chase Horner
PRIM
PRIM (Predictive Return on Investment Model) is a service that utilizes a Long Short-Term Memory Neural Network to predict a stock’s price in the future. The user has the option to view the most promising stock, to predict a stock’s price in an inputted amount of days, or to train and predict their own stock’s price data.
